# 3D Printing Club at the University of Utah

U of U 3D4E (3D for Everyone) is dedicated to empowering students with 3D printing mastery, fostering creativity, innovation, and making a positive impact. Our goals include:

- **Educational Excellence:** Elevating understanding and proficiency through workshops and projects.
  
- **Innovation and Creativity:** Encouraging boundary-pushing in manufacturing and design.
  
- **Community Impact:** Using our skills to solve real-world problems and contribute to society.
  
- **Collaboration and Interdisciplinary Learning:** Partnering with clubs and organizations for interdisciplinary growth.
